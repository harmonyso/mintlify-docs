## About Apple Business Manager

Apple Business Manager is Apple's platform for deploying and managing Apple devices at scale. It provides zero-touch enrollment, automated app distribution, and streamlined device management for organizations. Connecting Apple Business Manager to Harmony enables automated device provisioning and intelligent fleet management.

## What the Apple Business Manager integration enables

| Capability                    | Description                                                                                     |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Device Enrollment             | Automate device enrollment through DEP                                                          |
| Zero-Touch Deployment         | Enable automatic device configuration on first boot                                             |
| App Distribution              | Manage VPP licenses and app assignments                                                         |
| Device Assignment             | Assign devices to users automatically                                                           |
| MDM Integration               | Coordinate with MDM solutions (Jamf, Kandji, Intune)                                           |
| Purchase Tracking             | Track device purchases and serial numbers                                                       |

## Prerequisites

- **Apple Business Manager Admin**: Administrator or Device Enrollment Manager role
- **MDM Solution**: Connected MDM (Jamf, Kandji, or Intune)
- **Server Token**: Generated for MDM integration
- **Harmony Account**: Active Harmony workspace

## Configuration

### In Apple Business Manager

1. Sign in to [business.apple.com](https://business.apple.com)
2. Go to **Settings** > **MDM Server Assignment**
3. Assign devices to your MDM server
4. Configure default device enrollment settings

### In Harmony

1. Connect your MDM solution first (Jamf, Kandji, or Intune)
2. Harmony will automatically detect ABM-managed devices
3. Configure automated assignment rules
4. Set up new hire device provisioning workflows

## What Harmony Syncs

**From Apple Business Manager:**
- Device serial numbers and models
- Purchase dates and order information
- Device enrollment status
- MDM server assignments
- VPP license availability

**To Apple Business Manager:**
- Device assignment changes (via MDM)
- User assignments for devices

## Use Cases

- **Zero-Touch Onboarding**: New hire receives device, powers on, all apps and settings configured automatically
- **Bulk Device Deployment**: Order 50 MacBooks, automatically assign to new employees
- **VPP License Management**: Track and assign app licenses based on roles
- **Device Lifecycle**: From purchase through deployment to retirement
